About Us

Are you looking for the perfect place to get your nails done? Look no further! Our beauty nail shop in Ngara Nairobi has everything you need to feel pampered and polished.

Our experienced nail technicians are here to provide you with top-quality nail services, including pedicure, manicure, nail art, paraffin wax treatment, and acrylic nails. We use only the highest-quality products and equipment to ensure that your nails look their best and last as long as possible.

At our beauty nail shop, we believe that everyone deserves to feel beautiful and confident. That's why we offer a range of services to suit every style and budget. Whether you're looking for a quick touch-up or a full-on nail makeover, we have you covered.

So why wait? Book your appointment today and experience the ultimate in nail care. Our friendly and professional staff are ready to make you feel relaxed and rejuvenated. Come and see us in Ngara Nairobi and let us take care of all your nail needs!

Services

Acrylic nails

Applying a liquid and powder mixture to the natural nails, which hardens to create a durable and long-lasting finish

Gel nails

Applying a gel product to the natural nails and curing it under a LED lamp.

Manicure

Nail shaping, cuticle care, hand massage, nail polish and hand soak.

Nail art

Using nail polish and stickers to create intricate designs on the nails

Paraffin wax treatment

Dipping the hands or feet in warm melted wax to moisturize and soothe the skin.

Pedicure

Nail shaping, cuticle care, foot soak, foot massage, and application of nail polish

Shellac nails

Nail polish that is cured under a UV or LED lamp, resulting in a long-lasting and chip-resistant finish
